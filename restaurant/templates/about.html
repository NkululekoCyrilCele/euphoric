About Us

Euphoric is a family-owned restaurant, based in Johanneburg, Gauteng, whose focus is to serve palatable food and drinks.

The chefs have travelled the world to learn how to prepare top-quality dishes from different cultures and thus have a broad menu for all seasons.

The restaurant have an exuberant atmosphere with top-of-the-line dishes, attracting well-dressed patrons.

The restaurant was established by Makhosazane Hlongwa who has now been joined by his family to help run it and keep it to the high-standard.

The 15 chefs, including Makhosazane, that the restaurant has have taken their time to cook and learn how to prepare top-notch recipes from all around the world.

Makhosazane's husband only specialises in marketing for the restaurant, whereas she leads the effort to expand the menu to cater for the international audience of clients.
